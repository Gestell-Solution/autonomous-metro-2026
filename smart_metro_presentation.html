Core Concept & Architecture

Time-Based Delays

Trains moving solely based on pre-calculated software delays. Prone to desynchronization and unsafe states.

Status: Rejected

Event-Driven Distributed Logic

  • Local Autonomy: Train ECUs process local safety without waiting for central commands.
  • Global Supervision: Central ECU processes asynchronous events from Station nodes.
  • Deterministic: Behavior is dictated by physical states and RTOS priority scheduling.
